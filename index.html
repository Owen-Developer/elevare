FAQs
Frequently Asked Questions
Find quick answers to common questions about Elevare, our video services, process, and creative approach.
What types of videos do you edit?
We work on everything from short & long form social media content and corporate films to weddings, and creative projects.
How long is the editing process?
Turnaround time depends on the length and complexity of your project, but most edits are delivered within 4-6 business days.
Do I need to provide the footage?
Yes — we’ll need your raw clips, but we can also add licensed stock footage, music, and graphics to enhance your video.
How much does video editing cost?
Pricing varies based on the project’s length and complexity. Contact us with your idea, and we’ll provide a tailored quote.
How many revisions are included?
We offer up to 3 rounds of revisions to make sure the final video meets your expectations.
